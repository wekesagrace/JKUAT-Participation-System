Why SautiYetu

The smarter way to participate

Traditional feedback methods are slow, manual, and often ignored. SautiYetu changes that.

AI-Powered Analysis

Every submission is automatically analysed for sentiment, topic, and keywords — no manual reading required. Administrators see insights in real time.

Works on Any Device

Submit via web browser, SMS, or voice recording. No smartphone needed — a basic phone is enough. Inclusive by design, for every Kenyan.

Safe & Anonymous

Submit without your name. All data is encrypted, anonymised before storage, and protected under Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019.

Live Dashboards

Administrators see real-time charts, sentiment trends, and topic breakdowns — turning raw feedback into clear, actionable governance intelligence.

Voice Transcription

Speak your feedback in English or Swahili and the system transcribes it automatically using AI. Perfect for barazas and community meetings.

Instant Alerts

When negative sentiment spikes, administrators are alerted immediately — ensuring urgent issues are never missed or delayed.
